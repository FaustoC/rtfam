# FEALTY TO THE CROWN

###  If a player controls the Crown Zone and plays Fealty To The Crown, how many additional wounds does the enemy unit suffer and how many additional wounds are restored to a friendly unit?


 1 additional wound will be dealt and therefore only 1 additional wound will be restored.
